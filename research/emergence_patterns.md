# Asymmetric Emergence Patterns Research

## Key Concepts from Wikipedia

**Emergence** occurs when complex systems exhibit properties that individual components do not possess. There are two types:

- **Weak emergence**: Properties predictable from lower-level rules given enough computation
- **Strong emergence**: Properties irreducible to lower-level analysis

Example domains: cellular automata, ant colonies, market systems
